The Combat modules are designed to give you small, unnoticeable advantages in combat. With the right settings, these modules will help you crush your opponent completely undetected.

AimAssist
Smoothly aims to close range targets.
AutoClicker
Automatically clicks for you while holding down the attack button.
Reach
Increases your attack distance.
Velocity
Reduces knockback taken.
Sprint
Automatically activates sprinting for you.
WTap
Automatically WTaps for you.

Whether it be Players, Blocks, or anything else — Render modules provide you with the ability to see things you normally wouldn't, with ease.

Search
Renders an outline of blocks through walls.
NameTags
Renders name plates of entities through walls.
Tracers
Draws a line to entities on screen.
StorageESP
Draws an ESP around storage blocks.
Fullbright
Removes darkness.

For tedious and time sensitive tasks, Utility modules automate things quickly and efficiently.

Scaffold
Assists with bridging.
ChestSteal
Takes items upon opening a chest.
Blink
Chokes packets until disabled.
Refill
Refills your hotbar with healing items.
Throwpot
Throws or consumes healing items upon using keybind.

Besides just the modules, 91NE has several other unique and convenient features built in.

Internal GUI
GUI rendered inside your game window.
Profiles
Configurations that let you save multiple sets of different settings.
Friends
Adding friends prevents modules from targeting them.
Macros
Set macros to quickly swap to hotbar items.
Text GUI
Shows a list of all currently active modules on screen.
